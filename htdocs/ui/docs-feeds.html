An "feed" for an organisation is generally one of the following:

  • Events related to the organisation.
  • News items from and about the organisation (This does not include sites which provide an actual news service). eg. Vice Chancellor opens new building.
  • Notices from the organsiation. eg. Building 23 will be closed on May 4th for essential maintenance.

There may be other kinds of feed.

iCalendar data is probably not going to be news or notices, but please still mark it as events for easy discovery.

Prefixes

The examples on this page require the following prefixes to be defined at the top of your OPD:

@prefix dcterms: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/> @prefix oo: <http://purl.org/openorg/>

Example

<ORG-URI> a <http://purl.org/xtypes/Document-FEED-TYPE> oo:organization <ORG-URI> ; dcterms:subject <http://purl.org/openorg/theme/THEME> .

FEED-TYPE is one of: RSS or Atom or iCalendar

THEME is one of: news or events or notices
